Key Warnings

Stepper Motors

This system was designed and tested for the stepper motors in the table below. Use of other stepper motors may cause system failures.

The color of the Ethernet cable should match the color of the end cap on the stepper motor, and the jack on the Rosetta base unit.

NEMA 23 		2.4

The wiring design for the spindle does not support limit switches. Instead, two of the Ethernet wires are used for each stepper motor wire (i.e., brown & brown/white are used for A+).

The cable designated for the stepper motor for the spindle should be black, and should NOT be used for any other axes.
